About

We are a student organization of the College Of Computing at Georgia Tech.

We work on projects set by the group related to Big Data. No skill is needed to join the Org, and there are no membership dues. Make sure to check out our previous projects, sign up to our email list and see what we plan for the 2017-2018 academic year!
